Database

Here, you'll find a few samples of our database, with their respective GPS coordinates. Enjoy your tour of the Alps!

Our algorithm is quite simple. First, we find the skyline on your image using a Neural Network which solves a shortest path problem.
Then, we compare this skyline to the ones we computed on our database (we used GeoPose3K), and find the image which minimize the L2 distance between skylines.
